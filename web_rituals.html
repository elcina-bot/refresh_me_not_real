WEB RITUALS

Awakening. Vanishing. Loading.

AWAKENING

You wake with the glow of the screen. Notifications as modern prayer beads. Swipe. Tap. Refresh. You are now briefly alive.

VANISHING

You scroll until you dissolve. Every post replaces your body. You do not log out. You fade.

LOADING

A bar crawls across the screen, promising something new. Spinning wheels. Buffering icons. The sacred wait. The holy lag.

we never complete. we only load.
